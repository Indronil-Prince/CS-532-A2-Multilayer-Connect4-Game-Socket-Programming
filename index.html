About Me

I am currently enrolled in a PhD program in Computer Science at New Mexico State University located in Las Cruces, New Mexico, USA. I obtained a bachelor's degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Khulna University of Engineering and Technology (KUET) in Khulna, Bangladesh. I worked as a Wireless Network Engineer for Huawei Bangladesh and gathered experience many of the top telecommunication projects in Bangladesh like LTE Modernization, LTE TDD Deployment, 5G-NR Introduction. I also worked as a tool developer for data extraction, analysis and mining with Python in Huawei. I am passionate about coding with Python, C/C++, JavaScript. My research focuses on Machine Learning, Data Analysis, and Bioinformatics. I love understanding and reviewing codes on any programming language, a quick learner, a problem solver and ready to make a difference.
